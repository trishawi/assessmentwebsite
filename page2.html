Take deep breaths while listening to calm music. You can follow the instructions on breathing calmly, or just listen to the music.

Instructions:

  1. Find somewhere to sit or lie down comfortably.
  2. (optional) Play one of the soundtracks below.
  3. Close your eyes and breathe slowly, in and out.
  4. Focus on just the details of your breath and try not to think about anything else. If your mind strays away, go back to focusing on your breathing.
  5. When feeling more relaxed, slowly open your eyes.
